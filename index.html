The person behind the code

I'm a Computer Science student at Nirma University drawn to how systems fail — how data leaks, how APIs get abused, how a single misconfigured header opens the door. Understanding attack surfaces is the same thing as understanding the system, just from the other side.

My focus sits at the seam between backend engineering and cybersecurity: FastAPI and Flask for the systems I build, and log analysis, network threat detection, and ML-driven intrusion detection for the systems I audit. I've completed cyber job simulations with Tata, Mastercard, and Deloitte to sharpen the analyst side.

Before university, I represented Kuwait's U19 national cricket team and worked as an event photographer. Both taught me to stay composed under pressure and read a situation fast — the exact instincts that make a good incident responder.
